## How it works

### Control Mapping

Every Zentinelle policy maps to specific framework controls. Rate limits map to SOC 2 access controls. Content scanning maps to GDPR data protection. Audit logs map to everything.

See exactly which controls are satisfied and which have gaps.

### Continuous Monitoring

Compliance is not a point-in-time snapshot. Zentinelle continuously monitors policy compliance.

Drift detection. Real-time alerts. Remediation tracking. Your compliance posture is always current.

### Audit-Ready Reporting

Generate evidence packages for auditors. Export logs, policies, and control mappings in auditor-friendly formats.

Less scrambling before audits. Less back-and-forth with auditors. Less billable hours.
